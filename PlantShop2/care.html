Plant Care Tips

Learn how to keep your plants healthy and thriving!

Watering Tips

Make sure to water your plants properly. Overwatering can be just as harmful as underwatering!

Light Requirements

Different plants need different amounts of light. Be sure to know if your plant thrives in low, medium, or bright light.

Soil & Potting

Choose the right soil for each plant and ensure it has a pot with proper drainage.

Fertilization

Feed your plants with the right fertilizer to keep them growing strong and healthy!
